Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve? +
We primarily serve the Northern Cape region, with a focus on Kathu and surrounding areas. We can arrange delivery to other regions based on project requirements.
How does your B-BBEE Level 1 status benefit my company? +
As a 100% Black Female-owned company with B-BBEE Level 1 status, partnering with us allows your business to claim 135% of the value of your procurement spend with us on your B-BBEE scorecard, helping you meet your own empowerment goals.
What are your payment terms? +
Our standard payment terms are 30 days from invoice date. We also offer flexible payment options for larger projects and established clients.
How quickly can you respond to urgent requests? +
We understand that supply chain needs can be urgent. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, and we can often accommodate urgent requests with quick turnaround times.

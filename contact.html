Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to inquiries? +
We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at 072 970 7875.
What is your delivery area? +
We primarily serve the Northern Cape region, with a focus on Kathu and surrounding areas. We can arrange delivery to other locations based on order volume and requirements. Contact us to discuss your specific delivery needs.
Do you offer emergency delivery services? +
Yes, we offer emergency delivery services for established clients. Additional charges may apply for after-hours or emergency deliveries. Please contact us to discuss your urgent requirements.
How does your B-BBEE Level 1 status benefit my business? +
As a 100% Black Female-owned company with B-BBEE Level 1 status, partnering with us allows your business to claim 135% of the value of your procurement spend with us on your B-BBEE scorecard. This can significantly help you meet your own empowerment goals and compliance requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to? +
We primarily serve the Northern Cape region, with a focus on Kathu and surrounding areas. We can arrange delivery to other regions based on order volume and requirements.
What are your minimum order requirements? +
Minimum order requirements vary by product type. For fresh produce, we have a minimum order value of R500. For bulk and dry goods, minimums may be higher. Contact us for specific requirements based on your needs.
How do I place an order? +
Orders can be placed via phone, email, or through our website. We recommend placing orders at least 24 hours in advance for fresh products. Regular customers can set up standing orders for automated delivery.
Do you offer organic products? +
Yes, we offer a selection of organic produce and products. Availability may vary by season. Please contact us for current organic offerings and pricing.

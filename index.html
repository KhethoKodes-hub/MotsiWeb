About Motsi General Trading

Motsi General Trading is a trusted food supply and distribution company based in Kathu, Northern Cape. Founded in 2019, we specialize in providing high-quality food products to businesses and communities throughout the region.

As a 100% Black Female-owned enterprise with B-BBEE Level 1 status, we are committed to economic empowerment while delivering exceptional service and quality products to our clients.

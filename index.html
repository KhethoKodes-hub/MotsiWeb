About Motsi General Trading

Established in 2019 and based in Kathu, Northern Cape, Motsi General Trading (Pty) Ltd (Registration: 2019/432757/07) is a dynamic company focused on supply, distribution, and delivery services.

Led by Director Mrs. Kgomotsi Lekhobe, we are a 100% Black Female-owned enterprise committed to building strong business relationships and providing exceptional service to our clients. As a growing company, we understand the challenges businesses face and are dedicated to providing reliable solutions that help our partners thrive.

Our mission is to become a milestone-driven enterprise, renowned for our reliable supply chain solutions and commitment to excellence. While we are a startup with much to accomplish, our B-BBEE Level 1 status makes us an attractive procurement partner for larger enterprises.

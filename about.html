About Motsi General Trading

Building the future of supply chain solutions with integrity, excellence, and a commitment to empowerment

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Established in August 2019, Motsi General Trading began as a vision to create a reliable, ethical, and empowered supply chain solution provider in the Northern Cape region. Founded by Mrs. Kgomotsi Lekhobe, our company was built on the principles of integrity, quality service, and community development.

Though we are a relatively new player in the industry, our commitment to excellence and our unique status as a 100% Black Female-owned enterprise with B-BBEE Level 1 certification positions us as an ideal partner for businesses looking to strengthen their supply chains while supporting economic transformation.

Our journey is just beginning, and we are excited to build our legacy of success through dedication, hard work, and unwavering commitment to our clients' needs.

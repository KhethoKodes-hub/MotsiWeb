Our Story

Learn about Motsi General Trading's journey, values, and commitment to providing quality food products and services

From Humble Beginnings

Motsi General Trading was established in 2019 with a vision to provide quality food products and reliable distribution services to the communities of the Northern Cape. What started as a small family operation has grown into a trusted supplier for restaurants, retailers, and institutions throughout the region.

Founded by Mrs. Kgomotsi Lekhobe, our company was built on the principles of integrity, quality, and community service. We understand the importance of fresh, nutritious food and work tirelessly to ensure our clients receive the best products available.

As a 100% Black Female-owned enterprise, we are proud to contribute to economic empowerment while serving the community with excellence. Our B-BBEE Level 1 status reflects our commitment to transformation and creating opportunities.

Our Mission & Vision
